# Product Guidelines

## Voice and Tone
The "voice" of the channel is **enthusiastic, energetic, and high-octane**. Scripts should feel like they are being delivered by a passionate movie fan who is excited to share secrets. Sentences should be punchy, direct, and designed for fast-paced consumption.

## Visual Identity
- **Vibrant and Fast-Paced:** The editing style should be energetic, with frequent cuts and dynamic transitions.
- **High-Impact Overlays:** Use large, bold, high-contrast fonts (e.g., yellow text with black strokes) to ensure readability on small mobile screens.
- **Safe Zone Management:** All critical text overlays must be kept within the middle third of the screen to avoid interference with YouTube Shorts UI elements (Like, Dislike, Comments, and Channel info).

## Audio and Voiceover
- **Energetic Delivery:** The voiceover should be fast-paced and enthusiastic, matching the visual energy.
- **Shorts-Optimized Pacing:** The delivery must be efficient to ensure 5 distinct facts can be presented clearly within the 1:45 to 2:00 minute timeframe.

## Content Structure
- **Hook-Driven Intros:** Videos start with the movie poster and a high-energy hook: "Five things you didn't know about [Movie]!"
- **Engaging Selection:** The LLM should prioritize facts that are surprising, controversial, or technical secrets that are visually demonstrable through the movie clips.
